Description

Pav bhaji, or pao bhaji is a staple food of India, consisting of a thick spicy vegetable curry served with a soft, buttered bread roll. It is typically served either as a street snack or main dish, and originates from the city of Mumbai in Maharashtra.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 4 Butter pieces, finely chopped
  • 1 cup onion, chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup bottle gourd, chopped
  • 1/2 cup capsicum
  • 1 cup potato, chopped
  • 1/2 cup beetroot
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • 3 tsp pav bhaji masala
  • 1 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1/2 cup tomato puree
  • 1 cube butter
  • A bunch green coriander
  • Butter
  • Pav Bhaji Masala

Steps

  1. Oil in a pan. Add the onions with a piece of butter.
  2. Fry the onion until golden brown and add ginger garlic paste. Mix well.
  3. Now add green coriander with chopped gourd and mix well, then add a cup of chopped potatoes to it. Mash well. pav bhaji
  4. Add chopped beetroot, salt, red chilli powder and pav bhaji masala and mash it. Mix well.
  5. Now add the tomato puree.
  6. Mix the tomato puree and after adding the butter, add the green coriander and let the bhaji cook. Mix all the vegetables well.
  7. Spread the butter on the pav.
  8. Sprinkle pav bhaji masala on the pav.
  9. Cook the pav until golden brown in the pan.
  10. Serve hot with lemon slices, onions and green chillies.
